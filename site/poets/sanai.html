Persian · Mystical Classical

Sanai

c. 1080-1131 · Ghazni

Poetic signature

Sanai belongs to the Mystical Classical horizon of Persian literature. The defining concerns here are ethics, inward reform, spiritual satire, mystical awakening, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

सनाई की आवाज़ मनुष्य को भीतर से बदलने की पुकार है। वे बाहरी आडंबर पर व्यंग्य करते हुए आत्मशुद्धि और आध्यात्मिक सजगता को कविता का केंद्र बनाते हैं।

English interpretation

Sanai pushes Persian poetry inward. His verse critiques outward vanity and turns poetry into an ethical and mystical summons.

Why this poet matters

Sanai helps readers understand how Persian poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.
