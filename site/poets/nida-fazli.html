Urdu · Contemporary Urdu

Nida Fazli

1938-2016 · Delhi-Mumbai

Poetic signature

Nida Fazli belongs to the Contemporary Urdu horizon of Urdu literature. The defining concerns here are humanism, simplicity, urban solitude, reflective lyric, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

निदा फ़ाज़ली की पहचान सादगी में छिपी गहराई है। वे शहर, अकेलेपन और इंसानी रिश्तों को बिना बनावट की भाषा में व्यक्त करते हैं।

English interpretation

Nida Fazli practices lucid humanism. His poems turn urban solitude and ordinary relationships into meditative lyric moments.

Why this poet matters

Nida Fazli helps readers understand how Urdu poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.
