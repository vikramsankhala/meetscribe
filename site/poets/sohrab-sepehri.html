Persian · Modernist Persian

Sohrab Sepehri

1928-1980 · Iran

Poetic signature

Sohrab Sepehri belongs to the Modernist Persian horizon of Persian literature. The defining concerns here are nature, quietude, perception, spiritual simplicity, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

सोहराब सेपेहरी की कविता देखने की कला सिखाती है। प्रकृति और मौन उनके यहाँ आध्यात्मिक विस्तार और भीतरी शांति के रूप में आते हैं।

English interpretation

Sohrab Sepehri cultivates a poetics of seeing. Nature, stillness, and inward clarity become modes of spiritual attention.

Why this poet matters

Sohrab Sepehri helps readers understand how Persian poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.
