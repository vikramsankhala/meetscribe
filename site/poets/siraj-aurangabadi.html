Urdu · Early Urdu

Siraj Aurangabadi

1715-1763 · Aurangabad

Poetic signature

Siraj Aurangabadi belongs to the Early Urdu horizon of Urdu literature. The defining concerns here are mysticism, ecstasy, wandering self, inward fire, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

सिराज औरंगाबादी की कविता में सूफ़ियाना तल्लीनता और आत्मिक बेचैनी साथ मिलती हैं। वे प्रारंभिक उर्दू शायरी को गहरे आध्यात्मिक अनुभव से भरते हैं।

English interpretation

Siraj Aurangabadi infuses early Urdu poetry with ecstatic inwardness. His lyric world feels both wandering and incandescent.

Why this poet matters

Siraj Aurangabadi helps readers understand how Urdu poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.

Ten public-domain samples

These excerpts are presented in transliterated form for readability across audiences, with parallel English and Hindi renderings.

Sample 1

The wandering self

Khana ba dosh-e ishqam o dar ba daram hanuz.

English: I still wander door to door, carrying love upon my shoulders.

Hindi: मैं अब भी प्रेम को कंधों पर उठाए दर-दर भटकता हूँ।

Sample 2

The burning heart

Dil ra ba atish-e shauq jalaye rakha.

English: I kept the heart lit with the fire of longing.

Hindi: मैंने दिल को तड़प की आग से जलाए रखा।

Sample 3

Mystic bewilderment

Hairat ne mujhe apne hi andar khona sikhaya.

English: Wonder taught me how to lose myself within myself.

Hindi: हैरानी ने मुझे अपने भीतर ही स्वयं को खोना सिखाया।

Sample 4

The fakir's road

Faqiri rahe to saltanat khak shud.

English: Once fakiri appeared, worldly sovereignty turned to dust.

Hindi: फ़क़ीरी आते ही सांसारिक बादशाहत धूल हो गई।

Sample 5

Night of zikr

Shab bhar tera hi nam dua ban ke raha.

English: All night your name remained upon me like a prayer.

Hindi: सारी रात तुम्हारा नाम मुझ पर दुआ की तरह बना रहा।

Sample 6

The self undone

Bekhudi se raaz-e hasti khula.

English: Through self-forgetting, the secret of existence opened.

Hindi: बेख़ुदी से अस्तित्व का रहस्य खुल गया।

Sample 7

A burning dervish

Siraj is bazm mein khak nahin, shola hai.

English: Siraj in this gathering is not dust but flame.

Hindi: सिराज इस महफ़िल में धूल नहीं, लौ है।

Sample 8

The absent beloved

Hijr ne dil ko sukun se dur rakha.

English: Separation kept the heart far from repose.

Hindi: जुदाई ने दिल को चैन से दूर रखा।

Sample 9

The silent cry

Faryad bhi andar hi andar uthti rahi.

English: The cry kept rising, though only within.

Hindi: फरियाद उठती रही, पर भीतर ही भीतर।

Sample 10

Path of inward fire

Rah-e dil mein dhuan bhi tha aur nur bhi.

English: On the heart's path there was both smoke and light.

Hindi: दिल की राह में धुआँ भी था और रोशनी भी।
