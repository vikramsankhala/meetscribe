Urdu-Persian · Philosophical Modern

Muhammad Iqbal

1877-1938 · Sialkot-Lahore

Poetic signature

Muhammad Iqbal belongs to the Philosophical Modern horizon of Urdu-Persian literature. The defining concerns here are selfhood, awakening, civilizational thought, spiritual ascent, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

इक़बाल की शायरी आत्म-साक्षात्कार और सामूहिक जागरण की बड़ी पुकार है। वे फ़लसफ़े, रूहानियत और कार्रवाई को एक साथ जोड़ते हैं।

English interpretation

Iqbal’s poetry is visionary and exhortative. The self, history, and spiritual striving become dynamic energies for renewal.

Why this poet matters

Muhammad Iqbal helps readers understand how Urdu-Persian poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.

Ten public-domain samples

These excerpts are presented in transliterated form for readability across audiences, with parallel English and Hindi renderings. Orthography may vary slightly across editions.

Primary source trail

  • Bang-e Dara
  • Bal-e Jibril and public-domain Iqbal editions

Sample 1

Better than the world

Sare jahan se achchha Hindostan hamara.

English: Better than the whole world is our Hindustan.

Hindi: सारे जहाँ से अच्छा हमारा हिंदोस्ताँ है।

Sample 2

The nightingale and the garden

Ham bulbulen hain is ki, ye gulsitan hamara.

English: We are its nightingales, and this garden is ours.

Hindi: हम इसकी बुलबुलें हैं, और यह गुलिस्ताँ हमारा है।

Sample 3

Raise the self

Khudi ko kar buland itna ke har taqdir se pehle.

English: Raise the self so high that before destiny is written, God asks what you desire.

Hindi: अपने ख़ुदी को इतना ऊँचा करो कि हर तक़दीर से पहले ख़ुदा पूछे, तेरी रज़ा क्या है।

Sample 4

Before every decree

Khuda bande se khud puche bata teri raza kya hai.

English: So elevated should the human spirit become that God asks it for its will.

Hindi: मनुष्य की आत्मा इतनी ऊँची हो कि ख़ुदा स्वयं उससे उसकी इच्छा पूछे।

Sample 5

Stars beyond stars

Sitaron se age jahan aur bhi hain.

English: Beyond the stars are still more worlds to discover.

Hindi: सितारों के आगे भी और बहुत-सी दुनियाएँ हैं।

Sample 6

Trials of love

Abhi ishq ke imtihan aur bhi hain.

English: The tests of love are not over; many more remain.

Hindi: इश्क़ की परीक्षाएँ अभी समाप्त नहीं हुईं, अभी और भी बाकी हैं।

Sample 7

The eagle

Tu shahin hai, parwaz hai kam tera.

English: You are an eagle; your true work is flight.

Hindi: तुम शाहीन हो; तुम्हारा असली काम ऊँची उड़ान है।

Sample 8

Do not live low

Tere samne asman aur bhi hain.

English: Before you there are yet more skies to rise into.

Hindi: तुम्हारे सामने अभी और भी आकाश फैले हुए हैं।

Sample 9

Prayer of the child

Lab pe ati hai dua ban ke tamanna meri.

English: A prayer rises to my lips as the deepest desire of my heart.

Hindi: मेरे होंठों पर दुआ बनकर मेरे मन की तमन्ना आती है।

Sample 10

Illuminate life

Zindagi shamma ki surat ho khudaya meri.

English: O God, let my life be like a lamp, giving light to others.

Hindi: हे ईश्वर, मेरी ज़िंदगी शमा की तरह हो जो औरों को रोशनी दे।
