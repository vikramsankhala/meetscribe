Persian · Safavid

Saeb Tabrizi

1601-1677 · Tabriz-Isfahan

Poetic signature

Saeb Tabrizi belongs to the Safavid horizon of Persian literature. The defining concerns here are fresh conceits, moral reflection, Indian style imagery, wit, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

साएब तबरिज़ी की पहचान नए और अप्रत्याशित बिंबों से है। वे चिंतन और चमत्कार को इस तरह जोड़ते हैं कि ग़ज़ल में ताज़गी बनी रहती है।

English interpretation

Saeb Tabrizi delights in fresh turns of thought. His poetry renews the ghazal through agile imagery and reflective wit.

Why this poet matters

Saeb Tabrizi helps readers understand how Persian poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.
