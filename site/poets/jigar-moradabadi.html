Urdu · Romantic Modern Urdu

Jigar Moradabadi

1890-1960 · Moradabad

Poetic signature

Jigar Moradabadi belongs to the Romantic Modern Urdu horizon of Urdu literature. The defining concerns here are wine, passion, cadence, emotional warmth, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

जिगर मुरादाबादी की शायरी तरन्नुम, रूमानी गर्मी और जज़्बाती फैलाव की शायरी है। उनका लहजा दिल पर सीधा असर डालता है।

English interpretation

Jigar Moradabadi’s poetry sings. Passion, intoxication, and rhythmic warmth make his ghazals resonant and memorable.

Why this poet matters

Jigar Moradabadi helps readers understand how Urdu poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.
