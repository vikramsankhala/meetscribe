Persian-Hindavi · Indo-Persian

Amir Khusrau

1253-1325 · Delhi Sultanate

Poetic signature

Amir Khusrau belongs to the Indo-Persian horizon of Persian-Hindavi literature. The defining concerns here are cultural fusion, music, devotion, riddling charm, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

अमीर ख़ुसरो भारतीय और फ़ारसी संसारों के बीच एक सेतु हैं। उनकी शायरी और संगीत परंपराएँ मिलकर बहुभाषी और बहुरंगी सौंदर्य रचती हैं।

English interpretation

Amir Khusrau embodies cultural confluence. His work braids Persian sophistication with Indic sound, devotion, and playful invention.

Why this poet matters

Amir Khusrau helps readers understand how Persian-Hindavi poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.

Sample 1

Zehaal-e miskin

Zehaal-e miskin makun taghaful, duraye naina banaye batiyan.

English: Do not ignore the helpless lover, turning your eyes away while speaking sweetly.

Hindi: बेबस आशिक़ की दशा से यूँ बेपरवाह मत हो, आँखें फेरकर मीठी बातें मत करो।

Citation: amir-khusrau-s01 · Khusrau's Persian-Hindavi lyrical corpus · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 2

Chaap tilak

Chaap tilak sab chhini re mose naina milaike.

English: One glance from you stole my identity, my adornment, my very self.

Hindi: तुम्हारी एक नज़र ने मेरी पहचान, मेरा श्रृंगार, मेरा सारा अस्तित्व छीन लिया।

Citation: amir-khusrau-s02 · Qawwali and Indo-Persian traditional anthologies · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 3

Aaj rang hai

Aaj rang hai ri ma, rang hai ri.

English: Today the whole being is dyed in color, intoxicated with spiritual joy.

Hindi: आज पूरा अस्तित्व रंग में डूब गया है, मानो रूहानी उल्लास से भर उठा हो।

Citation: amir-khusrau-s03 · Khusrau's Persian-Hindavi lyrical corpus · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 4

Kaahe ko byahi bides

Kaahe ko byahi bides re, lakh babul more.

English: Why was I married away to a distant land, O my father?

Hindi: बाबुल, मुझे पराए देश क्यों ब्याह दिया गया?

Citation: amir-khusrau-s04 · Qawwali and Indo-Persian traditional anthologies · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 5

Nizam piya

Nizam piya rang rangili, chunariya de mohe.

English: Beloved master Nizam, grant me the many-colored veil of grace.

Hindi: मेरे प्रिय निज़ाम, मुझे अपनी कृपा की रंग-बिरंगी चादर दे दीजिए।

Citation: amir-khusrau-s05 · Khusrau's Persian-Hindavi lyrical corpus · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 6

Man kunto Maula

Man kunto Maula, fa haza Ali-un Maula.

English: Whoever takes me as master, let Ali be known as master too.

Hindi: जिसका मैं मौला हूँ, उसके लिए अली भी मौला हैं।

Citation: amir-khusrau-s06 · Qawwali and Indo-Persian traditional anthologies · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 7

Khusrau darya prem ka

Khusrau darya prem ka, ulti wa ki dhaar.

English: Love is a river flowing against the current; whoever enters it is swept beyond the self.

Hindi: प्रेम ऐसी नदी है जिसकी धारा उलटी बहती है; जो इसमें उतरता है, वह अपने आप से आगे निकल जाता है।

Citation: amir-khusrau-s07 · Khusrau's Persian-Hindavi lyrical corpus · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 8

Gori sove sej par

Gori sove sej par, mukh par daare kes.

English: The fair one rests upon the bed, her face covered by loosened hair.

Hindi: सजनी शय्या पर सोई है, चेहरे पर बिखरे केश पड़े हैं।

Sample 9

Mohe apne hi rang

Mohe apne hi rang mein rang de, Nizam.

English: Dye me completely in your own color, O Nizam.

Hindi: ऐ निज़ाम, मुझे अपने ही रंग में पूरी तरह रंग दो।

Sample 10

Saqiya

Saqiya, baz aa ke der in bazm jahan digar ast.

English: Cupbearer, return, for this gathering of the world has changed again.

Hindi: साक़ी, लौट आओ, क्योंकि दुनिया की यह महफ़िल फिर बदल गई है।
