Urdu · Classical Urdu

Mirza Rafi Sauda

1713-1781 · Delhi-Lucknow

Poetic signature

Mirza Rafi Sauda belongs to the Classical Urdu horizon of Urdu literature. The defining concerns here are satire, qasida, grandeur, critique, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

सौदा की शायरी में व्यंग्य और भाषिक वैभव दोनों प्रखर हैं। वे सामाजिक और राजनीतिक विडंबनाओं को तीखी कलात्मकता से पकड़ते हैं।

English interpretation

Sauda couples rhetorical power with sharp satire. His verse expands Urdu’s range beyond romance into critique and public speech.

Why this poet matters

Mirza Rafi Sauda helps readers understand how Urdu poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.

Sample 1

Where are the patrons

Kahan rahe ahl-e karam dekhte chale jao.

English: Look how the people of generosity have vanished from sight.

Hindi: देखो, उदार लोग कैसे नज़रों से ओझल होते चले गए।

Citation: sauda-s01 · Kulliyat-e Sauda · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 2

Worldly pretence

Ajab nahi ke zamane ne phir dagha di ho.

English: It is no surprise if the world has deceived us once again.

Hindi: यदि दुनिया ने फिर धोखा दिया हो, तो इसमें कोई आश्चर्य नहीं।

Citation: sauda-s02 · Kulliyat-e Sauda · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 3

Satire on power

Shah ko shah se zyada hawa lagti hai.

English: Kings are often more intoxicated by pride than by wisdom.

Hindi: राजा अक्सर बुद्धि से अधिक अहंकार के नशे में रहते हैं।

Citation: sauda-s03 · Kulliyat-e Sauda · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 4

Ruin of manners

Adab ka nam bhi is bazm mein baqi na raha.

English: In this gathering even the name of courtesy scarcely remains.

Hindi: इस महफ़िल में अब शिष्टता का नाम भी मुश्किल से बचा है।

Citation: sauda-s04 · Kulliyat-e Sauda · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 5

A cracked order

Kharabi-e chaman ka gham bhi hum ko kam nahin.

English: The ruin of the garden grieves me no less than private sorrow.

Hindi: बाग़ की बरबादी का ग़म मुझे निजी दुख जितना ही सालता है।

The city laments

Hindi: दिल्ली की हालत पर चारों ओर विलाप है।

Praise turned critique

False grandeur

Sample 10

Remember the real

Haqiqat a'i to rang-e tamasha utar gaya.

English: When reality arrived, the spectacle lost its color.

Hindi: जब सच्चाई सामने आई, तमाशे का सारा रंग उतर गया।
