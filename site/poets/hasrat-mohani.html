Urdu · Romantic-Political Modern

Hasrat Mohani

1875-1951 · Mohan-Aligarh

Poetic signature

Hasrat Mohani belongs to the Romantic-Political Modern horizon of Urdu literature. The defining concerns here are ishq, freedom, devotion, rebellion, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

हसरत मोहानी के यहाँ इश्क़ और इंक़िलाब विरोधी नहीं बल्कि सहचर हैं। वे रूमानी कोमलता और राजनीतिक प्रतिबद्धता दोनों को एक आवाज़ में रखते हैं।

English interpretation

Hasrat Mohani shows that romantic longing and political commitment can inhabit the same lyric self. His voice is tender and defiant at once.

Why this poet matters

Hasrat Mohani helps readers understand how Urdu poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.
