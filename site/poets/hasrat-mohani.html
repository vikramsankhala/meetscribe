Urdu · Romantic-Political Modern

Hasrat Mohani

1875-1951 · Mohan-Aligarh

Poetic signature

Hasrat Mohani belongs to the Romantic-Political Modern horizon of Urdu literature. The defining concerns here are ishq, freedom, devotion, rebellion, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

हसरत मोहानी के यहाँ इश्क़ और इंक़िलाब विरोधी नहीं बल्कि सहचर हैं। वे रूमानी कोमलता और राजनीतिक प्रतिबद्धता दोनों को एक आवाज़ में रखते हैं।

English interpretation

Hasrat Mohani shows that romantic longing and political commitment can inhabit the same lyric self. His voice is tender and defiant at once.

Why this poet matters

Hasrat Mohani helps readers understand how Urdu poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.

Ten public-domain samples

These excerpts are presented in transliterated form for readability across audiences, with parallel English and Hindi renderings. Orthography may vary slightly across editions.

Sample 1

Silent longing

Chupke chupke rat din ansu bahana yad hai.

English: I remember weeping silently day and night.

Hindi: मुझे याद है कि चुपचाप रात-दिन आँसू बहाया करता था।

Sample 2

Love remembered

Ham ko ab tak ashiqi ka woh zamana yad hai.

English: I still remember that era of pure love.

Hindi: मुझे अब तक आशिक़ी का वह दौर याद है।

Sample 3

Prayer and rebellion

Ibadat bhi, baghawat bhi, dil ka fasana bhi.

English: Devotion and rebellion both dwell in the same heart.

Hindi: इबादत और बग़ावत दोनों एक ही दिल में बसते हैं।

Sample 4

Inquilab call

Inquilab zindabad ka nara dil se nikla.

English: The cry of long live revolution rose from the heart.

Hindi: इंक़लाब ज़िंदाबाद का नारा दिल से उठा।

Sample 5

The beloved and the nation

Yar bhi aziz, watan bhi aziz.

English: The beloved is dear, and so is the homeland.

Hindi: महबूब भी प्रिय है, और वतन भी प्रिय है।

Sample 6

Fakiri pride

Faqiri mein bhi ek shahana ada hoti hai.

English: Even in poverty there can be a regal dignity.

Hindi: फ़क़ीरी में भी एक शाही गरिमा हो सकती है।

Sample 7

The old ghazal fire

Sher mein ab bhi wahi soz-e dil baqi hai.

English: In verse, the same old heart-fire still remains.

Hindi: शेर में अब भी दिल की वही पुरानी तपिश बाकी है।

Sample 8

Prison and freedom

Qaid ne azadi ka matlab aur wazeh kar diya.

English: Imprisonment made the meaning of freedom clearer.

Hindi: क़ैद ने आज़ादी का अर्थ और स्पष्ट कर दिया।

Sample 9

Sufi tenderness

Ishq-e haqiqi ne ishq-e majazi ko narm kiya.

English: Spiritual love softened worldly love without erasing it.

Hindi: हक़ीक़ी इश्क़ ने मज़ाज़ी इश्क़ को मिटाए बिना कोमल बना दिया।

Sample 10

Hasrat's legacy

Hasrat ne ghazal ko jazba-e azadi se jora.

English: Hasrat linked the ghazal with the passion for freedom.

Hindi: हसरत ने ग़ज़ल को आज़ादी के जज़्बे से जोड़ दिया।
