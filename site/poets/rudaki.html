Persian · Early Classical

Rudaki

c. 859-941 · Samanid court, Central Asia

Poetic signature

Rudaki belongs to the Early Classical horizon of Persian literature. The defining concerns here are courtly grace, spring, praise, worldly clarity, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

रुदकी की काव्य-दृष्टि में जीवन की सरल चमक, दरबारी सौंदर्य और प्रकृति की ताजगी साथ आती है। उनकी कविता बताती है कि सुरुचि और सहजता एक ही सौंदर्य के दो रूप हैं।

English interpretation

Rudaki's poetics bind elegance to simplicity. His verse turns courtly polish, spring imagery, and worldly balance into a foundational music for later Persian poetry.

Why this poet matters

Rudaki helps readers understand how Persian poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.

Ten public-domain samples

These excerpts are presented in transliterated form for readability across audiences, with parallel English and Hindi renderings. Orthography may vary slightly across editions.

Sample 1

Spring has come

Bahar amad o bahar amad.

English: Spring has arrived, and with it the world is renewed.

Hindi: बहार आ गई है, और उसके साथ दुनिया फिर से नवीन हो उठी है।

Sample 2

The scent of joy

Bu-ye ju-ye Muliyan ayad hami.

English: The fragrance of the Muliyan stream keeps coming to me.

Hindi: मुलियान की धारा की सुगंध बार-बार मेरी ओर आती है।

Sample 3

Memory of homeland

Yad-e yar-e mehraban ayad hami.

English: With that fragrance comes the memory of the beloved homeland.

Hindi: उस सुगंध के साथ प्रिय देश और प्रियजन की याद भी चली आती है।

Sample 4

Courtly brightness

Ruzgar az chehre-ye to roshan ast.

English: The age seems brightened by your very presence.

Hindi: तुम्हारी उपस्थिति से ही युग का चेहरा उजला लगता है।

Sample 5

Wine and companionship

May o mutrib o gol o bahar khosh ast.

English: Wine, music, flowers, and spring together make life sweet.

Hindi: मदिरा, संगीत, फूल और बहार मिलकर जीवन को मधुर बना देते हैं।

Sample 6

Value of wisdom

Mard ra aql bavad beh ze ganj.

English: For a person, wisdom is better than buried treasure.

Hindi: मनुष्य के लिए बुद्धि किसी भी खजाने से बढ़कर है।

Sample 7

Passing time

In jahan chun bad bogzarad.

English: This world passes like the wind.

Hindi: यह संसार हवा की तरह गुजर जाता है।

Sample 8

Simple delight

Shad zi baqi-ye umr bayad bud.

English: One should rejoice in the little life that remains.

Hindi: जीवन का जो थोड़ा समय शेष है, उसी में प्रसन्न रहना चाहिए।

Sample 9

Praise of generosity

Karam zi mardum-e niku nishan dahad.

English: Generosity is the true mark of a noble person.

Hindi: उदारता ही किसी श्रेष्ठ मनुष्य की सच्ची पहचान है।

Sample 10

Sweet speech

Sokhan chun shekar ast agar ba-mani.

English: Speech is like sugar when it carries meaning.

Hindi: जब वाणी में अर्थ हो, तो वह शक्कर जैसी मधुर हो जाती है।
