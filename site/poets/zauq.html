Urdu · Delhi Classical

Sheikh Ibrahim Zauq

1789-1854 · Delhi

Poetic signature

Sheikh Ibrahim Zauq belongs to the Delhi Classical horizon of Urdu literature. The defining concerns here are courtly polish, idiom, discipline, craft, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

ज़ौक़ की पहचान सधे हुए शिल्प और ज़बान की पक्की पकड़ में है। वे उर्दू को दरबारी परिष्कार और बोलचाल की रवानगी दोनों देते हैं।

English interpretation

Zauq represents disciplined craftsmanship in Urdu. His command of idiom and balance of polish with fluency shaped a generation.

Why this poet matters

Sheikh Ibrahim Zauq helps readers understand how Urdu poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.
