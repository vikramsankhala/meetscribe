Persian · Late Classical

Jami

1414-1492 · Herat

Poetic signature

Jami belongs to the Late Classical horizon of Persian literature. The defining concerns here are sufi synthesis, grace, scholarship, devotional love, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

जामी की काव्य-दृष्टि में विद्वता और कोमलता साथ-साथ चलती हैं। वे सूफ़ियाना प्रेम को संतुलित, परिपक्व और सुंदर अभिव्यक्ति देते हैं।

English interpretation

Jami’s poetry is learned yet gentle. He gives late classical Persian a harmonizing voice where scholarship and devotion support each other.

Why this poet matters

Jami helps readers understand how Persian poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.

Ten public-domain samples

These excerpts are presented in transliterated form for readability across audiences, with parallel English and Hindi renderings.

Sample 1

Scholar and lover

Ilm agar ba ishq nabashad, hijab ast.

English: Knowledge without love becomes a veil.

Hindi: यदि ज्ञान में प्रेम न हो, तो वह परदा बन जाता है।

Sample 2

Joseph's beauty

Husn-e Yusuf jahan ra fitna kard.

English: Joseph's beauty set the whole world in wonder.

Hindi: यूसुफ़ का सौंदर्य पूरे जगत को विस्मय में डाल देता है।

Sample 3

Zulaikha's longing

Zulaikha ra talab ba jan rasid.

English: Zulaikha's longing reached the depth of the soul.

Hindi: ज़ुलेखा की तलब आत्मा की गहराई तक पहुँच गई।

Sample 4

Sufi sobriety

Dar mastiyat ham hosh bayad bud.

English: Even in ecstasy one must retain a higher awareness.

Hindi: उन्माद में भी एक ऊँची सजगता बनाए रखनी चाहिए।

Sample 5

Garden of meaning

Mani chu gol ast o lafz barg-e u.

English: Meaning is the flower, and words are its petals.

Hindi: अर्थ फूल है और शब्द उसकी पंखुड़ियाँ हैं।

Sample 6

Mirror of the friend

Dust dar ayina-ye dust khod ra did.

English: The friend saw himself in the mirror of the Friend.

Hindi: मित्र ने मित्र के आईने में स्वयं को देखा।

Sample 7

Grace of restraint

Narmi ze kamal ast na az zaf.

English: Gentleness comes from perfection, not weakness.

Hindi: कोमलता कमजोरी से नहीं, परिपूर्णता से आती है।

Sample 8

The path of unity

Rah yakist agarche suratha besyar.

English: The path is one, though forms may be many.

Hindi: राह एक ही है, भले रूप अनेक हों।

Sample 9

The breath of devotion

Zikr chun nafas jan ra safa bakhshad.

English: Remembrance purifies the soul like breath itself.

Hindi: स्मरण आत्मा को सांस की तरह निर्मल करता है।

Sample 10

Jami's counsel

Ba adab bash ke dar rah-e dusti rahbar ast.

English: Be courteous, for courtesy is the guide on the path of friendship.

Hindi: अदब में रहो, क्योंकि मित्रता की राह में वही मार्गदर्शक है।
