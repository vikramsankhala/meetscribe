Urdu · Late Classical Urdu

Insha Allah Khan Insha

1756-1817 · Delhi-Lucknow

Poetic signature

Insha Allah Khan Insha belongs to the Late Classical Urdu horizon of Urdu literature. The defining concerns here are wit, linguistic play, experimentation, elegance, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

इंशा भाषा के खेल और शहरी नफ़ासत के बड़े उस्ताद हैं। उनकी शायरी बताती है कि बुद्धि, विनोद और शैली भी ग़ज़ल के महत्वपूर्ण आयाम हैं।

English interpretation

Insha Allah Khan Insha revels in language itself. Wit, experimentation, and social polish animate his contribution to Urdu lyric culture.

Why this poet matters

Insha Allah Khan Insha helps readers understand how Urdu poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.

Sample 1

Wit in language

Insha ne zaban ko bhi tamasha bana diya.

English: Insha turned language itself into a delightful performance.

Hindi: इंशा ने भाषा को ही एक मनोहर तमाशा बना दिया।

Citation: insha-s01 · Kulliyat-e Insha · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 2

A playful address

Tum ham se bolo pyar se, ham bhi jawab dein.

English: Speak to me with affection, and I will reply in kind.

Hindi: तुम मुझसे प्रेम से बोलो, मैं भी वैसा ही उत्तर दूँगा।

Citation: insha-s02 · Late-Delhi Urdu anthologies · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition
