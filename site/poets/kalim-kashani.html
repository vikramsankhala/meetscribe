Persian · Safavid-Mughal

Kalim Kashani

1581-1651 · Kashan-India

Poetic signature

Kalim Kashani belongs to the Safavid-Mughal horizon of Persian literature. The defining concerns here are ornament, delicacy, migration, subtle conceits, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

कलीम काशानी की कविता में भाषा का सजाव और कल्पना की महीन कारीगरी विशेष रूप से दिखती है। वे प्रवास और दरबारी संस्कार दोनों को सौंदर्य में बदलते हैं।

English interpretation

Kalim Kashani excels in delicate ornament. His verse reveals how migration and refinement can reshape poetic texture.

Why this poet matters

Kalim Kashani helps readers understand how Persian poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.
