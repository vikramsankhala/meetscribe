Persian · Safavid-Mughal

Kalim Kashani

1581-1651 · Kashan-India

Poetic signature

Kalim Kashani belongs to the Safavid-Mughal horizon of Persian literature. The defining concerns here are ornament, delicacy, migration, subtle conceits, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

कलीम काशानी की कविता में भाषा का सजाव और कल्पना की महीन कारीगरी विशेष रूप से दिखती है। वे प्रवास और दरबारी संस्कार दोनों को सौंदर्य में बदलते हैं।

English interpretation

Kalim Kashani excels in delicate ornament. His verse reveals how migration and refinement can reshape poetic texture.

Why this poet matters

Kalim Kashani helps readers understand how Persian poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.

The Person

Kalim Kashani appears here not only as a literary figure, but as a reflective craftsperson who turns lived experience into memorable language. Behind the verse is a recognizable human temperament shaped by Safavid-Mughal pressures, Kashan-India realities, and recurring concerns such as ornament, delicacy, migration, subtle conceits.

For contemporary generations, this is a poet to relate to as much as to admire: someone negotiating identity, love, loss, power, faith, doubt, and dignity in language that still feels emotionally current.

If they lived today: a thoughtful essayist-poet, helping readers make sense of complexity with grace and precision.

Sample 1

Ornamented lyric

Har lafz chu zar dar narm silk-e sukhan ast.

English: Each word is like gold threaded in soft silk of verse.

Hindi: हर शब्द कविता के रेशमी वस्त्र में पिरोया हुआ सोना है।

Citation: kalim-kashani-s01 · Diwan-e Kalim Kashani · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 2

The migrant voice

Az Kashan ta Hind dilam hamrah-am bud.

English: From Kashan to Hindustan, my heart traveled with me.

Hindi: काशान से हिंदुस्तान तक मेरा दिल मेरे साथ चला।

Citation: kalim-kashani-s02 · Diwan-e Kalim Kashani · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 3

The painterly line

English: I drew the image of meaning with a single stroke.

Hindi: मैंने अर्थ की तस्वीर एक ही रेखा से खींच दी।
