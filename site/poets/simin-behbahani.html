Simin Behbahani

1927-2014 · Iran

Poetic signature

Simin Behbahani belongs to the Contemporary Persian horizon of Persian literature. The defining concerns here are renewed ghazal, social courage, womanhood, civic tenderness, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

सिमीन बेहबहानी ने ग़ज़ल को समकालीन अनुभवों के लिए फिर से जीवित किया। उनकी कविता में सामाजिक साहस और मानवीय कोमलता साथ रहते हैं।

English interpretation

Simin Behbahani renews the ghazal from within. Traditional cadence carries contemporary conscience, gendered experience, and civic courage.

Why this poet matters

Simin Behbahani helps readers understand how Persian poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.

The Person

Simin Behbahani appears here not only as a literary figure, but as a reflective craftsperson who turns lived experience into memorable language. Behind the verse is a recognizable human temperament shaped by Contemporary Persian pressures, Iran realities, and recurring concerns such as renewed ghazal, social courage, womanhood, civic tenderness.

For contemporary generations, this is a poet to relate to as much as to admire: someone negotiating identity, love, loss, power, faith, doubt, and dignity in language that still feels emotionally current.

If they lived today: a thoughtful essayist-poet, helping readers make sense of complexity with grace and precision.
