Persian · Modern Persian

Parvin Etesami

1907-1941 · Iran

Poetic signature

Parvin Etesami belongs to the Modern Persian horizon of Persian literature. The defining concerns here are ethical dialogue, social conscience, dignity, education, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

परवीन एतेसामी की कविता नैतिक संवाद और सामाजिक संवेदना की कविता है। वे गरिमा, शिक्षा और न्याय को बहुत स्पष्ट और प्रभावी स्वर देती हैं।

English interpretation

Parvin Etesami restores ethical urgency to modern Persian verse. Her poems speak with lucidity, compassion, and a strong civic conscience.

Why this poet matters

Parvin Etesami helps readers understand how Persian poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.
