Persian · Epic Classical

Ferdowsi

c. 940-1020 · Tus, Greater Khorasan

Poetic signature

Ferdowsi belongs to the Epic Classical horizon of Persian literature. The defining concerns here are epic memory, kingship, fate, civilizational continuity, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

फ़िरदौसी के यहाँ कविता केवल कहानी नहीं बल्कि सभ्यता की स्मृति है। वीरता, नैतिकता और समय की चोट को वे ऐसे जोड़ते हैं कि इतिहास आत्मा की कथा बन जाता है।

English interpretation

Ferdowsi makes poetry a vault of civilization. In his epic imagination, heroism, justice, and mortality become instruments for preserving a people’s moral memory.

Why this poet matters

Ferdowsi helps readers understand how Persian poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.
