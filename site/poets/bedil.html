Persian · Sabk-e-Hindi

Abdul Qadir Bedil

1644-1720 · Delhi

Poetic signature

Abdul Qadir Bedil belongs to the Sabk-e-Hindi horizon of Persian literature. The defining concerns here are abstraction, metaphysical density, inward labyrinths, imagination, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

बेदिल की कविता विचार और अनुभूति के जटिल गलियारों में चलती है। वे अर्थ को प्रत्यक्ष नहीं बल्कि सूक्ष्म संकेतों, बिंबों और दार्शनिक घनत्व में रचते हैं।

English interpretation

Bedil is a master of metaphysical complexity. His verse thrives on compressed images, abstraction, and intellectual astonishment.

Why this poet matters

Abdul Qadir Bedil helps readers understand how Persian poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.

Sample 1

The world as imagination

In jahaan khayal ast o ma khayal andar khayal.

English: This world is imagination, and we are imagination within imagination.

Hindi: यह संसार कल्पना है, और हम कल्पना के भीतर की कल्पना हैं।

Citation: bedil-s01 · Diwan-e Bedil (public-domain manuscripts/editions) · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 2

Mirror of bewilderment

Ayina-am ke hayratam az aks-e khodast.

English: I am a mirror astonished by my own reflection.

Hindi: मैं ऐसा आईना हूँ जो अपने ही प्रतिबिंब पर चकित है।

Citation: bedil-s02 · Diwan-e Bedil (public-domain manuscripts/editions) · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 3

Dust and cosmos

Az gard-e rah to afaq paida shod.

English: From a single grain of dust, horizons emerge.

Hindi: राह की एक धूल-कण से भी क्षितिज जन्म ले लेते हैं।

Citation: bedil-s03 · Diwan-e Bedil (public-domain manuscripts/editions) · Public-domain compiled edition · various (19th-early 20th c.) · p. varies by edition

Sample 4

Self and disappearance

Hasti be fana mi rasad az rah-e shenakht.

English: Being reaches annihilation by the path of deep knowing.

Hindi: अस्तित्व गहन पहचान की राह से फ़ना तक पहुँचता है।

A breath of paradox

Hindi: सांस स्वयं बंधन भी है और मुक्ति भी।

Bedil's signature

Bedil sukhan ra rah-e tajrid ast.

English: For Bedil, speech is a path toward abstraction and unveiling.

Hindi: बेदिल के लिए वाणी अमूर्तन और अनावृत्ति की राह है।
