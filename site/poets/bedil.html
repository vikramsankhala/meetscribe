Abdul Qadir Bedil

1644-1720 · Delhi

Poetic signature

Abdul Qadir Bedil belongs to the Sabk-e-Hindi horizon of Persian literature. The defining concerns here are abstraction, metaphysical density, inward labyrinths, imagination, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

बेदिल की कविता विचार और अनुभूति के जटिल गलियारों में चलती है। वे अर्थ को प्रत्यक्ष नहीं बल्कि सूक्ष्म संकेतों, बिंबों और दार्शनिक घनत्व में रचते हैं।

English interpretation

Bedil is a master of metaphysical complexity. His verse thrives on compressed images, abstraction, and intellectual astonishment.

Why this poet matters

Abdul Qadir Bedil helps readers understand how Persian poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.
