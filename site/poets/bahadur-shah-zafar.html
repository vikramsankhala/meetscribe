Bahadur Shah Zafar

1775-1862 · Delhi-Rangoon exile

Poetic signature

Bahadur Shah Zafar belongs to the Late Mughal Urdu horizon of Urdu literature. The defining concerns here are exile, fading sovereignty, sorrow, remembrance, giving the poet a durable place within the long afterlife of sher o shayari.

Hindi interpretation

ज़फ़र की कविता राजनीतिक पराजय को गहरे मानवीय दुख में बदल देती है। उनके यहाँ बादशाहत की परछाईं से अधिक निर्वासन की तन्हाई बोलती है।

English interpretation

Zafar’s verse is haunted by loss of home and empire. The poignancy of exile turns his poetry into a document of historical heartbreak.

Why this poet matters

Bahadur Shah Zafar helps readers understand how Urdu poetry balances image, emotion, philosophy, and historical feeling. This folio page situates the poet within the broader movement from classical craft to modern and global continuities.
