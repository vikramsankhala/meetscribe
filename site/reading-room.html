How to read this atlas

Interpretation, not literal translation

The Hindi and English sections offer literary readings of each poet’s style, atmosphere, and contribution rather than strict line-by-line translations.

Read comparatively

Move between Mir and Hafez, Ghalib and Bedil, Faiz and Shamlu, Forugh and Parveen Shakir to notice continuities and departures.

Use the poet index

The index lets you browse by era and tradition, making the site useful for both teaching and leisurely exploration.

Curated as a panoramic literary museum of Urdu and Persian poetics, from foundational voices to digital-era continuities.
